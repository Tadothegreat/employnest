What is Blind Screening?

Blind screening evaluates candidates based only on their skills and qualifications, ignoring demographic information like name, age, gender, or location. This helps reduce unconscious bias in the hiring process and promotes diversity and inclusion.

Note: Scores are calculated solely from skills and qualifications text. Personal identifiers are hidden during this evaluation.
